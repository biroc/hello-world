Jean-Michel Jarre

Composer - Performer - Producer

  • 1948 - born as Jean-Michel André Jarre on 24 August in Lyon, France
  • 1972 - wrote music for the International Festival of Magic
  • 1972 - released his first solo album, Deserted Palace
  • 1976 - first mainstream success with the album Oxygène
  • 1979 - free concert for more than a million people at the Place de la Concorde
  • 1981 - first western musician officially invited to perform in China
  • 1986 - Rendez-vous Houston: Performance for Texas's 150th anniversary
  • 1990 - concert for two million people in Paris on Bastille Day
  • 1995 - 50th anniversary of the UN: Concert for Tolerance
  • 1997 - Moscow: largest ever outdoor concert with 3.5 million people
  • 2000 - released his first vocal album, Métamorphoses
  • 2006 - Water for Life concert to celebrate the UN Year of Desertification
  • 2016 - part of the advisory panel of the Democracy in Europe Movement 2025
